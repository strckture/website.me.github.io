Twitter Insight

How much does Twitter know about you?
This project was created together with Max Herrmann to remind users that the internet never forgets.
If you enter your twitter handle the website will display various types of information about yourself. You can get a glance of how much is stored online and could easily be recovered.With the help of this project we hope to start a thinking process about what to share on social media.
Sadly due to restrictions in the Twitter API the website could not be published and has to remain just a reminder to be careful with your personal data on the Internet.

Flotes GUI

Flotes is a Webapp designed to support your learning progress.
A lot of students still have to learn vocabulary and simple facts. Often flash notes are used to accomplish that. But flashnotes are very unwieldy for everyday use. So the idea of flotes is to digitalize those flash notes to make it a lot easier for you to carry them around.
This is a digital prototype for the GUI of www.flotes.de. The actual site is still under construction.
